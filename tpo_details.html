Worked on a Web App that would serve as an automation for the Training and Placement Cell of a College.
Students can upload resumes, update their profiles, find opportunities, practise, give mock tests and discuss things that matter.
This project was started for a Hackathon in which my team reached till the final round.
Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node JS, PostgreSQL
